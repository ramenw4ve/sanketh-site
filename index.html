Welcome to Sanketh - For Better Life...!

Sanketh Social Awareness Organization is a registered society under the Registrar of Societies Hyderabad (South), Government of Telangana. We are aiming for 12A registered society with section 80(G) status of income tax. Our financial records are audited annually by Saachi Associates. Financial efficiency is one of unique strengths of Sanketh model. We developed a delivery network of volunteers across various cities and villages in India to deliver services to society with prime focus of students at free of cost. Every program in Sanketh is created and serviced with non profit mode and principled to serve in a nonprofit service nature.

Our Projects

Career Awareness Program (CAP)

The CAP program broadens awareness on career options and ensures that students know about various post-school study, financial aids, skill developments and career opportunities available and understand how to access and achieve them.

Details

Road And Street Traffic Awareness (RASTA)

RASTA creates awareness on the best practices, methods and measures pedestrians, cyclists, motorists need to follow for road safety to prevent serious injury and death crashes in road mishaps.

Details

Make A Difference (MAD)

In MAD program, Sanketh aimed to facilitate a medium between people and government to gather plans and solutions from people which revolutionize the changes in policies and guidelines in any of the systems for a better society tomorrow.

Details

Camps on Health Awareness and Social Education (CHASE)

Sanketh organizes awareness program CHASE towards creating a healthier society by popularizing, networking preventive measures on various diseases, health and hygiene issues with the help of lectures.
